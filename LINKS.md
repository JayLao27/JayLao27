# Where to put your social links

All **clickable links** (Resume, Website, LinkedIn, etc.) live in **`readme.md`**.  
Replace each `href="..."` with your real URL. The images are generated by your Worker; only the links below need editing.

---

## 1. Worker URL (used in every image)

**File:** `readme.md`  
**Replace:** Every `https://YOUR_SUBDOMAIN.workers.dev` with your real Worker URL.

The Worker name is set in **`wrangler.toml`** as `name = "jaylao"`. After you run `pnpm wrangler deploy`, your URL will be something like `https://jaylao.workers.dev`. Use that full URL (with your actual subdomain) in every `srcset` and `img src` in `readme.md`.

---

## 2. Resume (PDF or link)

**File:** `readme.md`  
**Find:**  
`<a target="_blank" href="YOUR_RESUME_LINK">`  
**Put your link in:** `href="..."`  

Example: `href="https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/YOUR_REPO/blob/main/files/Your-Resume.pdf?raw=true"`  
Or any URL where your resume PDF is hosted.

---

## 3. Personal website

**File:** `readme.md`  
**Find:**  
`<a target="_blank" href="YOUR_WEBSITE">`  
**Put your link in:** `href="..."`  

Example: `href="https://yourname.dev"`

---

## 4. LinkedIn

**File:** `readme.md`  
**Find:**  
`<a target="_blank" href="YOUR_LINKEDIN">`  
**Put your link in:** `href="..."`  

Example: `href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/yourusername/"`

---

## 5. Facebook

**File:** `readme.md`  
**Find:**  
`<a target="_blank" href="YOUR_FACEBOOK">`  
**Put your link in:** `href="..."`  

Example: `href="https://facebook.com/yourusername"`  
To hide this link, delete the whole `<a>...</a>` block for Facebook.

---

## 6. Instagram

**File:** `readme.md`  
**Find:**  
`<a target="_blank" href="YOUR_INSTAGRAM">`  
**Put your link in:** `href="..."`  

Example: `href="https://www.instagram.com/yourusername"`

---

## 7. Twitter / X

**File:** `readme.md`  
**Find:**  
`<a target="_blank" href="YOUR_TWITTER">`  
**Put your link in:** `href="..."`  

Example: `href="https://twitter.com/yourusername"` or `https://x.com/yourusername`

---

## Summary

| Link       | In readme.md        | What to change              |
|-----------|---------------------|-----------------------------|
| Worker URL | All image `src`/`srcset` | `YOUR_SUBDOMAIN.workers.dev` → your real Worker URL |
| Resume    | First `<a href="...">` | `YOUR_RESUME_LINK` → your resume URL |
| Website   | Second `<a href="...">` | `YOUR_WEBSITE` → your site URL |
| LinkedIn  | Third `<a href="...">` | `YOUR_LINKEDIN` → your LinkedIn URL |
| Facebook  | Fourth `<a href="...">` | `YOUR_FACEBOOK` → your Facebook URL (or remove block) |
| Instagram | Fifth `<a href="...">` | `YOUR_INSTAGRAM` → your Instagram URL |
| Twitter/X | Sixth `<a href="...">` | `YOUR_TWITTER` → your Twitter/X URL |

Edit **only** the `href="..."` values (and the Worker URL in image tags). Leave the rest of the HTML as is so the SVG images still load from your Worker.
